Welcome to Poski's World

About Me

Hi! I'm Poski, a 24-year-old mechatronics engineer passionate about technology, cars, and running. Originally from Guadalajara, Jalisco, I graduated with a double degree from Tec de Monterrey and Hochschule Zittau/Gorlitz, with an outstanding bachelor thesis done in Munich, Bavaria, in collaboration with BMW, where I explored the fascinating world of software verification and validation for on-board standardization of vehicle signals.

Currently, I work as an application development engineer in the automotive industry, with a particular focus on braking projects, while nurturing my curiosity and passion for systems and software. My long-term goal is to become an expert in networking, cloud, and cybersecurity.

Interests

When I'm not immersed in the tech world, I find myself exploring new paths in my running shoes. This year, I challenged my limits with my first 30k trail run, and I'm aiming to run my first 42k marathon in November. In addition to my interests in engineering and sports, I enjoy immersing myself in topics such as personal finance, business, psychology, physics, and ethics.

Reflections

Introspection and writing are integral parts of my life. Through my website, I aspire to share my reflections, tools, and insights on a variety of topics that I am passionate about. My goal is to create a space where I can share my experiences, ideas, and learnings with others who are passionate about personal and professional growth.
