About Me

Hi there!

I'm Poski, a 24-year-old mechatronics engineer passionate about technology, cars, and running. Originally from Guadalajara, Jalisco, I graduated with a double degree from Tec de Monterrey and Hochschule Zittau/Gorlitz, with an outstanding bachelor thesis done in Munich, Bavaria, in collaboration with BMW, where I explored the fascinating world of software verification and validation for on-board standardization of vehicle signals. Currently, I work as an application development engineer in the automotive industry, with a particular focus on braking projects, while nurturing my curiosity and passion for systems and software. My long-term goal is to become an expert in networking, cloud, and cybersecurity.
